Who We Are

        Backpack Buddies of Maple Valley (WA) is a non-profit organization that
serves students in the Tahoma School District. Our vision is to help create a
community where nutritional needs of all Tahoma students are met and stu-
dents are able to thrive and be successful at home, at school and in extracurric-
ular activities.

Who We Serve

        We serve students who face nutritional insecurity over the weekend be-
cause they are lacking sufficient nutrition at home or are unsure where their
next meal will come from. Students families enroll voluntarily and their identi-
ty is only known to the counselor at their school.
