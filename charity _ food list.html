Food List

        Our goal is to supplement food the student has at home. Knowing that living
conditions may vary, we provide nonperishable food items along with fresh fruit.
We also wish to provide items that are easily prepared. Backpack Buddies runs on
donations alone and look for sales and deals when purchasing our food. Brands may
change, but overall ingredients in the bag remain the same.
  • macaroni and cheese
  • canned soup or pasta
  • chocolate milk
  • juice
  • popcorn
  • applesauce or fruit cups
